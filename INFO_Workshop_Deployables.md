### SalJam_Workshop_Deployables

  - Adds lategame WorkShop Deployables.
  - An Exotic Scanner that has a 4x larger scan area, Exotic and uranium extractors that are twice as fast and have 18 slots.
  - A solar panel that gives 20k power and a battery with 15M capacity and 20k flow rate.
  - A heater, cooler and dehumidifer that give an increaded radius.
  - Much larger refillable Oil, Water and Biofuel containers from the workshop.
  - An ore diluter that combines ores with stone, creating higher quantities of ore.
  - Diluter includes a recipe for Lithium Ore, Crystalized Ruby and allows for cnverting Red Exotic to purple.
  - Increases drop ship inventory from 15 to 20 to allow for more items to start on a drop.
  - Moved deployables to its own section at the bottom of the workshop list.
